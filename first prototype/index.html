  • About

    This application utilizes a Machine Learning model to offer an alternative to the traditional methods of species classification. This model will quickly predict, with a high degree of accuracy, the ant species found in the uploaded images reducing the time and expertise usually needed for classification.

    This system will be useful for documenting and digitizing natural history specimens from museum collections around the world and will help foster citizen science by empowering amateur naturalists to identify insects in the field.
